From Lead Chaos to Automated Closings

📅 July 2026⏱ 5 min read
🏠

How a 5-agent team in Denver tripled their showing bookings

Lead response time: 8-12 hours → 60 seconds · Conversion: 6% → 22% · Revenue: +$60K/mo

The Client

Company: Alpine Realty Group (pseudonym)
Location: Denver, CO
Team size: 5 agents + 1 admin
Annual transactions: ~80 homes
Challenge: Inbound lead overload with no system for follow-up

The Problem

Alpine Realty was generating 200+ leads per month through Zillow, Realtor.com listings, Facebook ad campaigns, and their website's "Schedule a Showing" form. But their conversion rate was stuck at 6%. Here's why:

  1. Slow response time: Leads that came in overnight or during showings weren't contacted until the next day — by then, many had booked with someone else
  2. Unqualified leads: 40% of the leads they called back were window-shoppers with no timeline, no budget, or looking in a different area
  3. CRM abandonment: They used HubSpot but nobody logged calls or follow-ups consistently
  4. Admin bottleneck: The single admin was spending 20+ hours/week just on initial lead responses

"Our lead flow was great — we just couldn't keep up. It felt like we were drowning in inquiries while the real opportunities slipped through." — Team Lead

The Solution

Alpine Realty implemented Coreforge Engineering's Full Stack Suite ($997/mo):

Phase 1 — AI Chatbot (Week 1)

  • Installed chatbot on their website and Zillow presets
  • Chatbot asks: budget range, timeline, areas of interest, property type
  • Unqualified leads (no budget, >6 months out) go to a nurture sequence
  • Qualified leads get an auto-reply with the agent's calendar link

Phase 2 — Email Automation (Week 2)

  • Set up automated follow-up sequences for non-responders
  • Past client nurture (anniversaries, market updates, referral requests)
  • Post-showing follow-up sequence

Phase 3 — CRM Integration (Week 3)

  • HubSpot integration: every chatbot conversation auto-creates a contact
  • Deals auto-created for qualified leads
  • Showings logged automatically

Key Takeaways

  1. Speed matters most: Responding in seconds instead of hours was the single biggest factor
  2. Pre-qualification saves time: Chatbot filtered out 40% of leads before a human ever touched them
  3. Automation doesn't replace agents: It lets them focus on what they do best — showing homes and closing deals

"I was skeptical about a chatbot handling our leads, but the numbers don't lie. We went from scrambling to keep up to having a system that works while we sleep. I'd recommend it to any team that's outgrowing manual follow-up." — Team Lead, Alpine Realty Group
