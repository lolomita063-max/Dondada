$45k/Month in Recovered Revenue

📅 July 2026⏱ 5 min read

How an HVAC company captured after-hours leads and booked 12 extra jobs per week

After-hours capture: 0% → 85% · New monthly revenue: $45K · Google reviews: 12 → 87

The Client

Company: Mile High HVAC (pseudonym)
Location: Phoenix, AZ
Team size: 4 trucks, 8 employees (2 office + 6 field)
Annual revenue: ~$1.8M
Challenge: Missing after-hours calls and losing customers to competitors

The Problem

Mile High HVAC was a growing business with a reputation for quality work. But they had a critical weakness:

  1. 40% of calls came after hours: Evening and weekend calls went to voicemail. By morning, customers had already called three other companies
  2. No booking system: Customers had to call during business hours to schedule — inconvenient and friction-filled
  3. Follow-up was nonexistent: Past customers never heard from them again, missing seasonal maintenance and tune-up revenue
  4. No review collection: Despite doing great work, they had only 12 reviews on Google — their competitors had 200+

"I knew we were losing jobs. A customer would call at 8 PM with a broken AC in Phoenix summer, leave a voicemail, and by 7 AM they'd already booked someone else. That's $300+ walking out the door every time." — Owner

The Solution

Mile High HVAC started with Lead Gen Pro ($297/wk) and added AI Chatbot Pro ($497/mo):

Phase 1 — Chatbot + Auto-Booking (Week 1)

  • Chatbot installed on website + Google Business Profile messaging
  • Handles: "My AC isn't working" → asks address → asks about issue → offers available slots
  • Books appointments directly into their service calendar using Schedule for Good
  • After-hours: chatbot operates fully autonomously

Phase 2 — Email Automation (Week 2)

  • Automated seasonal maintenance reminders (spring AC tune-up, fall furnace check)
  • "We haven't seen you in a year" re-engagement emails
  • Post-service thank-you + review request sequence

Phase 3 — Review Automation (Week 3)

  • After every completed job: automated text → "How was your service?"
  • 5-star response → Google review link
  • Less than 5-star → alert owner for personal follow-up

Key Takeaways

  1. "After hours" doesn't mean "after opportunity": An AI that works 24/7 captures revenue that was previously lost
  2. Past customers are gold: Automated re-engagement brought back 34% of past clients for seasonal service
  3. Reviews drive business: Going from 12 to 87 reviews increased their local search ranking and trust factor
